yi ~ Normal(mu_i, sigma)

S = (sigma_alpha, 0, 0, sigma_beta)_2x2 R (sigma_alpha, 0, 0, sigma_beta)_2x2
alpha ~ Normal(0, 10)
beta ~ Normal(0, 1)
sigma ~ HalfCauchy(0, 2)
